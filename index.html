About me

Who am I?
I'm a thirty something woman working towards a career in computer programming. I love the challenge coding brings and the following satisfaction on having found a solution to the problem at hand. Outside of tech I enjoy all things homestead, fiber arts, getting into the great outdoors of the Pacific Northwest where I reside, and spending time with my family.
I've worked in several different professions and for the past 9 years have been doing personal support work for adults with developmental disabilities. My goal is to secure employment where I'm still able to help others while being able to express my creative side.
